**# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students**

**✅ Do's**
- Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal information
- Think before posting any content
- Share positive and meaningful content
- Verify information before sharing
- Maintain a professional online presence

**❌ Don'ts**
- Do not share personal details like address or passwords
- Avoid posting offensive or harmful content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Do not overshare private information
