# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist 

Staying safe online is very important, especially as we use the internet daily for studies, payments, and social media. Here are some practical tips that every college student should follow:

## ✅ Safety Tips

- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts. Avoid using your name or date of birth.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible for extra security.
- Do not share personal information like OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
- Be careful while clicking on links received through emails, SMS, or social media.
- Always verify unknown calls or messages claiming to be from banks or companies.

## 💰 Financial / UPI Safety

- Never approve unknown “collect requests” on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
- Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers, especially when receiving money.
- Always double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment.

## 📱 Social Media Awareness

- Avoid posting sensitive personal information publicly.
- Do not accept friend requests from unknown people.
- Think before posting anything that could affect your reputation.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime

If you become a victim of any cyber fraud or suspicious activity:

- Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

## ✔️ Final Note

Being alert and careful online can save you from major problems. A little awareness goes a long way in staying safe in the digital world.
